Books

Let us be grateful to live in a time where we can benefit from revolutionary innovations in technology as well as learn from the great stories that have tested time. I love how epic it can feel to read a book in the the night or morning, when it's really quiet and the world is waking up or going to sleep. In a world filled with short-form content, reading is a flex-worthy habit (especially non-fiction and history).

Below, you can find the books that I am currently reading, am planning on reading, or have finished reading. I'd love to have discussions about any of these books so definitely reach out if you've finished or plan to read any of these.
